Welcome to the tutorial page!

Here you can learn the basics of how to use the Oversight website

How to manage your transactions

When you have successfully logged in, you will be directed to the logged in page where you can select any of the following:

  • See transactions
  • See spending plan
  • My Account

Select "See Transactions" to see graphical information on your spending habits, as well as add new spending data!

Here you can add spending data and view graphical information on spending habits!

To add a new transaction, simply click on the "Add a new transaction" button, select a category from the drop down menu, input the amount and date of purchase, then finally click the "Add Transaction" button to add the transaction to the system.

If you feel you've made an error, you can then simply delete any transaction with a click of the delete button.

How to manage your spending plan

Next, try clicking on the "See spending plan" button located in the top right of the screen between "See transactions" and "Tutorial Page". If you do not already have a spending plan, try creating a new one by clicking on the "Create new spending plan" button. Then simply input your estimated planned expenses per category and the website will display it visually for your convenience.

Select the link to create a spending plan!

Input your desired planned expenses per category

Click "Create Spending Plan" at the bottom to finalize the spending plan.

Congratulations, you have created your first spending plan!
